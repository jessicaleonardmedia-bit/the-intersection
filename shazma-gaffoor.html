About

Experienced broadcast professional and journalist with expertise in news production, content creation, and live media operations.

Skilled in story development and on-camera presentation across television and digital platforms, Shazma brings a commitment to diversity and inclusion with strong news gathering and reporting capabilities. As a community-minded storyteller with extensive contacts across multicultural communities, she focuses on amplifying underrepresented voices and telling stories that matter.

With experience spanning broadcast journalism, digital media, and community reporting, Shazma combines rigorous research with compelling narrative craft to explore social justice, cultural identity, and the intersection of policy and lived experience.
