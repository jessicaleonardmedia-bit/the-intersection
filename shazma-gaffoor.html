About

Shazma Gaffoor is a journalist and writer who tells complex and compelling stories about social issues.

Her writing spans health research reporting, international affairs, and investigative features, with a particular focus on public health policy, cultural identity, and community. Shaz translates complex research into accessible, engaging stories for diverse audiences exploring the intersection of policy, lived experience, and systemic change.

Her writing spans health research, international affairs, and investigative features across digital and academic platforms. She brings nuanced perspectives to underrepresented voices, translating complex research into accessible, engaging stories.
