About

The Intersection is a panel talk show that explores issues through an unapologetically intersectional lens. Covering topics from gender and sport, food and culture, the media industry, the politics of pleasure, gender and disability, and menstruation, The Intersection will bring you honest stories and expert analysis from perspectives we don't usually hear, using a multicultural and gender-diverse lens.

Vision

A kind and tolerant society that shares compassion with people from all walks of life.

Mission

To create community television that reaches the hearts and minds of everyday people, breaks down ignorance, and fosters everyday kindness.

Values

Kindness – At the heart of everything we do.

Respect – We value the experiences of all people.

Inclusivity – We make space for those often left out.

Intersectional Feminism – We uplift people in an inclusive way.

Authenticity – We stay true to ourselves, our values and beliefs.

Gentle Curiosity – Openness and a desire to understand.

Optimism – Believing in the possibility of a better, fairer future.

Bravery – Willing to challenge norms and speak truth with care.

Unashamed – Proud of who we are and the stories we share.

Our Hosts

Producers and Hosts Shazma Gaffoor and Jessica Leonard first met at La Trobe University, in 2018. They were both mums of school aged children, retraining to join the media industry. Despite having very different backgrounds and upbringings, they've formed a strong friendship. Jess and Shaz love having deep conversations about big ideas.
