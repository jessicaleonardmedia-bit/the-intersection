About

The Intersection is that conversation where you finally get real.

Gender, sport, food, culture, bodies, periods. All the stuff that matters, told by the voices mainstream media keeps missing. Nothing's off limits, everyone brings something different to the table.

It's the chat you wish you were having more often.

Vision

A kind and tolerant society that shares compassion with people from all walks of life.

Mission

To create community television that reaches the hearts and minds of everyday people, breaks down ignorance, and fosters everyday kindness.

Our Hosts

On paper, an unlikely match. Shaz is a third-culture kid - born in Sri Lanka, raised in Hong Kong, home in Australia. Jess is a proud bogan who's never left Australia.

But from the moment they met at uni, the only journalism students who were also mums raising kids, they spent hours laughing and talking.

Turns out, the best conversations happen when perspectives complement each other.

They teamed up with Producer and Director Sarah Lewis to create The Intersection, a panel talk show bringing multicultural and gender-diverse voices to the issues that matter.
