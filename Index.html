About The Intersection

The Intersection is a community television show that brings local stories, perspectives, and voices to the forefront. We believe in the power of diverse viewpoints and the importance of looking at issues from multiple angles.

Our tagline "Look Both Ways" embodies our commitment to presenting balanced, thoughtful coverage of the topics that matter to our community.

Our Mission

To create meaningful dialogue and connection within our community through authentic storytelling and inclusive programming.
